Formal hypothesis
This atlas is framed as a computational formalization, not a theorem-proof. Thoughts, feelings, goals, and intentions are modeled as state transitions in a partially free dynamical field. Precision is operationalized as a measured function of low layout energy, activation coherence, receiver quality, and an intention field whose moral valence can flip under empathy tuning.
